Simple Machinery

A port to 1.16.5 is currently being worked on.

It adds simple machines, that are highly configurable, to Minecraft. All machines, except the Autoclave, have no default recipes, as they are primarily meant for pack developers to customise to their own needs.


Machines

  • Autoclave
  • Fluid Centrifuge
  • Sieve

Autoclave

  • Fuel: Steam (Input via top face)
  • Input: Item + Liquid
  • Output: Item

Fluid Centrifuge

  • Fuel: Forge Energy
  • Input: Liquid
  • Output: Items + Liquids

Turntable

  • Fuel: Forge Energy
  • Input: Item
  • Output: Item

Default Recipes

  • Concrete Power + 125 mB Water -> Concrete (25 ticks, 5 mB/t Steam)
